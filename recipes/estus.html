Estus Flask: (Dark Souls)

Description:

Dark Souls is a video game both famous and infamous for unapologetically difficult combat and a sprawling interconnected world, all of which is cohesively designed to make the world of Lordran feel as isolating and unwelcoming to the player as it is to their character. Set in a cold and fading world where players fight through mindless undead, imposing knights, and grotesque demons, the most comfort one can find is in the game's reliable health-restoring Estus Flask. Here's one recipe that imagines the warm incandescent orange draught as a strong drink that burns fiery with citrus notes which will certainly remind you to Praise the Sun on the way down.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Estus Flask (glass bottle, for serving)
  • 6 oz Pulp-Free Orange Juice
  • 6 oz Ginger Beer
  • 2 shots Cinnamon Whiskey
  • 3 Ice Cubes
  • Squeeze of Lemon

Steps:

  1. Blend orange juice, lemon juice, ice and whiskey in a blender.
  2. Pour mixture into the serving bottle. You may need to use a funnel, depending on your bottle.
  3. Top off with ginger beer and try not to die.
